Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To craft exquisite fragrances that embody elegance, individuality, and self-expression. Vanna Marie Perfume is dedicated to providing high-quality, unique scents that elevate personal style and enhance confidence, with every spray capturing a story of beauty, sophistication, and authenticity.

Vision

To be a leading name in the fragrance industry, inspiring a world where personal scent is an art form that celebrates individuality and enriches daily life. We envision Vanna Marie Perfume as a symbol of elegance and quality, known for its commitment to crafting memorable scents that resonate with people from all walks of life.

Our Best Seller

Vanna Marie - Enchanted Rose

Description: Enchanted Rose is a soft, romantic fragrance that captures the essence of femininity. The opening notes of citrus lead into a heart of delicate rose and jasmine, creating a beautiful, sophisticated bouquet. The base notes of sandalwood and amber add warmth and elegance, making it a perfect scent for both day and night wear. Why It’s Special: This scent exudes timeless elegance with a modern twist, ideal for women who appreciate classic floral notes with a soft, powdery finish.

Vanna Marie - Velvet Orchid

Description: Velvet Orchid is a captivating, bold scent that blends the richness of fruits with the delicate, exotic florals of orchid and jasmine. The sweet, slightly spicy base of vanilla and tonka bean provides an alluring depth, while patchouli adds an earthy element to the fragrance. Why It’s Special: A scent for the modern woman, Velvet Orchid is perfect for evening wear, offering both complexity and warmth that lingers throughout the night.

Vanna Marie - Ocean Breeze

Description: Ocean Breeze is a refreshing and invigorating scent that captures the essence of the open sea. The citrusy freshness of grapefruit and lemon is balanced with the herbaceous notes of lavender and sage. The woody base of cedarwood and sandalwood makes it sophisticated yet invigorating, ideal for a fresh, daily wear. Why It’s Special: This scent is perfect for active, outdoorsy men who enjoy refreshing and clean fragrances with a touch of elegance.

Vanna Marie - Midnight Leather

Description: Midnight Leather is a bold and masculine fragrance that embodies strength and sophistication. The opening notes of black pepper and bergamot lead into a heart of rich leather and cedarwood, while the base notes of amber and musk provide warmth and depth, creating a signature scent that is perfect for evening wear or special occasions. Why It’s Special: Midnight Leather exudes confidence and charm, making it ideal for men who want a powerful yet refined scent for a night out or a formal event.
